Research

My current focus is on network data and low-rank structure with non-uniform observation patterns, including propensity estimation and robust matrix completion.

  • Network inference: latent position models, graph embeddings, and inference under sampling/measurement constraints.
  • Matrix completion: nuclear norm / SVT-style estimators, inverse propensity weighting, and theory/algorithms under missing not at random.
